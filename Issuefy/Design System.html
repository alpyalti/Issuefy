Issuefy · Design System v1.1

The system behind a calm, credible brief.

Issuefy turns a noisy market into one trustworthy read. The visual language is built to feel premium, editorial and quiet: black, white and warm grays, a single calm blue, and a clear division of labor between three typefaces. Motion is purposeful, every screen is responsive to the phone, and the components below make it all feel like one product.

07

Motion & interaction

Motion is calm and purposeful, never decorative. It signals state and guides attention, and always yields to reduced-motion.

Glass island header

Flat and full-width at the top; on scroll it lifts into a floating, frosted island.

Ambient hero

A soft field of blue blinds with a spotlight that follows the cursor on the hero.

Signal flow

One light travels monitor → summarize → verify, then loops seamlessly.

Edge glow

Cards bloom a subtle blue border toward the cursor on hover — never on touch.

Scroll tilt

The product preview tilts back in 3D and rotates flat as it scrolls into view.

cubic-bezier(.2,.7,.3,1)
150–420ms
reduced-motion safe

Principles

One easing curve, short durations, and a static fallback whenever reduced-motion is set.
